## 1. BRAND DNA

### Who We Are
**NEEMAN'S IS A CONSCIOUS FASHION BRAND.**

Not a "shoe company." Not a "sustainability brand." A **conscious fashion brand** — fashion-first, responsibility-built-in.

### Positioning Statement
> **Neeman's encourages you to make responsible lifestyle choices with conscious fashion that is trendy, innovative, and comfortable.**

### Positioning Breakdown
- **FOR:** The urban Indian audience between 21-45 yrs
- **WHO:** Have a taste for effortless style and would like to adopt a conscious lifestyle
- **NEEMAN'S IS:** Their responsible companion
- **THAT:** Brings fashion choices that are trendy, comfortable and innovative while being conveniently priced and conscious
- **UNLIKE:** Other fashion brands that are only about fast-changing designs or sustainable products that are high priced

### Tagline
**CHANGE THE NORM**

### Brand Vision
> To create a sustainable future built on a foundation of trust, transparency and responsibility.

### Brand Mission
> To become the most admired responsible Indian fashion brand.

### Brand Story
> We are on a journey across the country to explore its diversity, discover its aspirations and connect with its people and their aspirations. Our ambition is to soak in the essence of the country and in doing so, to capture the minds and hearts of the people we connect with along the way and share our passion for responsible fashion with them, so each one of them becomes part of the Neeman's community.

### Brand Essence — What It Does for People
| Layer | What It Delivers |
|---|---|
| **Functional Benefits** | Comfortable, lightweight, affordable, minimal/understated design, washable, sustainable/conscious |
| **Self-Expressive Benefits** | "When I buy or use this brand, I am a catalyst of change that does good" |
| **Emotional Benefits** | "It reaffirms me as a trendy change agent and I feel confident that I am looking good, feeling good and doing good" |

### Brand Values

| **FORWARD-THINKING** | Constantly innovating to meet consumer expectations and take responsibility for the planet's future |
| **POSITIVE CHANGE** | Every responsible act has the potential to bring about positive change in how we create and consume |

| Attribute | Description |
|---|---|
| **COMFORTABLE** | Materials, design and styling created to deliver never-before comfort that balances with fashion quotient |
| **FASHIONABLE** | Committed to raising consumers' fashion quotient with always-in-style trendy designs crafted to perfection |

| **RESPONSIBLE** | Constant innovation using sustainable materials and processes good for people and planet |

### The Three Communication Pillars
| Pillar | How to Talk About It |
|---|---|
| **RESPONSIBILITY** | A **personal choice** — self-reward. "When you choose Neeman's, you feel good about yourself." Talk about it as something the wearer does for themselves. |

| Area | How |
|---|---|
| **MATERIALS** | Responsible sourcing, sustainable materials |
| **PROCESSES** | Responsible manufacturing, transparency |

- Made from recycled, natural and sustainable materials
- Right-priced sustainable products
- Innovation in materials and processes
- Loyal customer base with positive product reviews
- Minimal design aesthetic

**Consumer Pain Points:**
- Sustainable products are priced high

**Competitor Strengths:**
- Lesser priced brands paying lip service to sustainability

- Top brands including Neeman's selling on discount

## 5. TONE OF VOICE

### Core Principle
> Our brand is **young and spontaneous, natural and original**. The tone of voice of our messaging has to reflect that, ALWAYS.

### The 4 Rules of Neeman's Copy

#### Rule 1: TALK, DON'T WRITE
Our communication is a conversation. People are more comfortable listening than reading.
- **Don't:** "Talking of care for your shoes, simply remove accumulated dirt with a clean shoe brush."
- **Do:** "It's easy to care for your shoes. Just use a clean shoe brush to remove dirt and dust."

#### Rule 2: ASK QUESTIONS
A question is a nudge. It asks the audience to react. Reaction is the first step of interaction.
- "Wouldn't it be great if your shoes didn't kill your feet at the end of the day?"
- "Because the planet is worth protecting, isn't it?"

#### Rule 3: AVOID CLICHES
We are not a cliche. The biggest cliches are adjectives. Paint pictures instead.
- **Don't:** "Breathtaking design"
- **Do:** "Design that will make the world gasp in admiration"
- **Don't:** "sky blue"
- **Do:** "Blue inspired by the sky"
- **Do:** "Green like the colour of envy"

#### Rule 4: USE U.S. ENGLISH
Not just a spelling choice — it's a cultural choice. American English is young, popular, casual, and conversational.

### How to Talk About The 3 Pillars

**RESPONSIBILITY (Personal Choice — Self-Reward):**
- "The earth needs Neeman's as much as you do."
- "Want to make a difference to the planet? Start right at the bottom — with your feet!"

**FASHION (Social Choice — External Reward):**
- "Shoes designed for you to walk. And to stop people in their tracks."
- "You'll have to get used to answering the question, 'Where did you get these from?'"

**COMFORT (Rational Choice — Describe the Experience):**
- "Your feet will thank you at the end of every day."
- "It's like walking barefoot on wet grass on a cool morning."
- DON'T just say "comfortable" — describe what wearing Neeman's FEELS like.

### Customer-Facing Voice
- **Speak the customer's language** — English, Hindi, and state-specific languages. Don't feel inaccessible.
- **Don't be overly formal** — casual yet respectful. Young, spontaneous, original.
- **Be fashion-forward** — able to hold conversations about trends, give pairing tips.
- **Expertise is second nature** — deep product knowledge, reassuring, NOT condescending.
- **Warm, friendly, approachable** — never irritated, rude or apathetic.
- **Customer context** — gauge age. Elderly = Sir/Ma'am. Younger = first name.

## 6. VISUAL IDENTITY SYSTEM

### Logo System
Neeman's has 4 logo variants:
1. **Brand Logo** — wordmark "NEEMAN'S"
2. **Logo with Symbol** — wordmark + icon
3. **Logo with Tagline** — wordmark + "CHANGE THE NORM"
4. **Brand Symbol** — icon only

### Logo Rules
- Logo appears ONLY in **black or white** — never any other color
- Clear space = "X" (height of the 'S' in the logo)
- Minimum print size: 2.5 cm (wordmark), 3 cm (with symbol), 3.5 cm (with tagline), 1.5 cm (symbol only)
- Minimum web size: 70 px (wordmark), 80-110 px (with symbol), 90-100 px (with tagline), 40 px (symbol only)
- **NEVER:** Change color, use outline, stretch/distort, change spacing, tilt, change format

### Typography

| Role | Font | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary Headline** | **Bebas Neue Pro Regular** | ALL CAPS only. Headlines in all communication. |
| **Secondary Headline** | **Bebas Neue Regular** | ALL CAPS. Used when thicker font needed for legibility, or when text interacts with images. |
| **Body Text** | **DIN Regular / Medium / Bold** | Body copy. Can be sentence case. DIN Medium may be used as headline if sentence case is required. |
| **Web Body** | **Roboto Regular / Medium / Bold** | Website body text (web-safe) |
| **Email Headline** | **Arial Narrow Regular / Bold** | Emailer headlines |
| **Email Body** | **Verdana Regular / Bold** | Emailer body text |

### Typography Rules
- Bebas Neue Pro Regular: **ONLY in ALL CAPS** for headlines
- If you need sentence case headlines, use **DIN Medium** (not Bebas)
- When text interacts with imagery (overlaid), use **Bebas Neue** (not Pro)
- Bebas Neue Pro and DIN should NOT be used overlaid on images
- Body copy always in **DIN Regular**

### Color Palette

#### Primary Colors (Nature-inspired, earthy tones)
| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|

| **Wool** | `#EDD8C3` | E-commerce backgrounds, warm neutral |
| **Dessert** | `#F0E6E1` | Soft backgrounds |
| **Pebble** | `#E9E1D6` | E-commerce backgrounds |

#### Secondary Colors (Brighter accents)
| Name | Hex Range (Light → Dark) |
|---|---|
| **Ocean** | `#B5D1D1` → `#96BFC1` → `#75AFB0` → `#649C9F` |
| **Periwinkle** | `#BCCBDF` → `#A0B9D6` → `#85A5CB` → `#7891C1` |
| **Coral** | `#E5C7BF` → `#DDB1A4` → `#D69B8A` → `#CD857C` |

| **Forest** | `#C4D2C3` → `#AEC3AA` → `#97B292` → `#80A27D` |

### Color Usage Rules

**Studio Shots:**
- Model/product shot against a brand color → text/callouts on a **white patch**
- Background can be extended from image color
- Patch can be **white** or **coal** — NEVER another beige/primary color on a colored background
- If image on beige background → patch is white or coal only

**E-Commerce:**
- Use **Wool** and **Pebble** as product backgrounds
- White shoes → always on Wool background
- Check contrast for other shoe colors
- **NEVER** use secondary colors for e-commerce backgrounds

**Secondary Colors with Models:**
- Model clothes MUST match background color OR be neutral (black/white)
- Shoes should be neutral (black/white) against secondary colors
- Colored shoes ideally on primary backgrounds. If on secondary → shoe color must match background

**Outdoor Campaign Shots:**
- Outdoor images paired ONLY with **coal or white**
- **NEVER** use primary or secondary color patches with outdoor campaign shots

**Sale Banners:**
- **Red typography** for banners using studio shots on solid color backgrounds
- **Red button** for banners using outdoor campaign shots

## 7. DESIGN FRAMEWORK — BANNER & AD CREATION

### Design System Overview
A performance marketing framework consisting of **image, text, and graphic components**. Design instructions:
- Elements have defined usage
- Spacing between elements is pre-defined
- **Headline is mandatory**, other elements are variable

### Design Elements
1. **Logo** — top corner
2. **Title/Headline** — primary text (Bebas Neue Pro, ALL CAPS)
3. **Subtext** — supporting copy (DIN)
4. **Image** — hero shot (model, product, on-feet, outdoor)
5. **Closeup** — product detail shot (optional)
6. **Button** — CTA (e.g., "SHOP NOW")

### Content Buckets (Photo Types)
| Type | Variants |
|---|---|
| **With Models** | Outdoor campaign shots, Studio shots |
| **Product on Feet** | Outdoor campaign shots, Studio shots |
| **Product Only** | Outdoor campaign shots, Studio shots |

### Margin System
- Margin = **size of the "S" in our logo** = X
- Square: 1/3X margins
- Vertical rectangle: 1/3X margins
- Horizontal rectangle: 1/5X margins

### Grid Layouts Available

#### Horizontal Rectangle
| Grid | Use Case |
|---|---|
| **3-module** | Brand ambassador/model + single product; New product launch + material detail; Outdoor shots + single product |
| **2-module** | Model + product alternate layout; Product on feet |
| **Free-flowing** | Closeup studio shots; Spaced typography (A I R Y / C O M F Y / C O N S C I O U S) |

#### Vertical Rectangle (Stories)
| Grid | Use Case |
|---|---|
| **3-module** | Outdoor campaign + single product; New product launch + material detail |
| **2-module** | Product on feet; New product launch with single product; Outdoor campaign with model |
| **Free-flowing** | Brand ambassador; Spaced typography on image |

#### Instagram Stories
| Grid | Use Case |
|---|---|
| **Free-flowing** | Model shots; Spaced typography overlays |
| **2-module** | Product on feet; Outdoor campaign; New product launch |

#### Square
| Grid | Use Case |
|---|---|
| **2-module** | New product launch; Product on feet; Brand ambassador |
| **Free-flowing** | Model shots; Spaced typography (THE BEST FITTING APPAREL) |

#### Horizontal Wide
| Grid | Use Case |
|---|---|
| **3-module** | Product on feet outdoor; Product range studio; Closeup studio; Product range free-flowing |

### Headline Copy Patterns (From Framework Examples)
**Spaced letterform style:**
- `A I R Y  C O M F O R T A B L E  C O N S C I O U S`
- `A I R Y  C O M F Y  C O N S C I O U S`
- `A I R Y, C O M F Y, P L A N E T  P O S I T I V E`

**Statement headlines:**
- PURE. STYLISH. EFFORTLESS
- WALK THE EARTH, LIGHTLY
- PLANET POSITIVE FASHION
- CONSCIOUS TOGETHER
- A COTTON HUG FOR YOUR FEET
- FIND THE PERFECT FIT
- LIGHT, CONSCIOUS, COMFORTABLE
- WE PUT A CORK IN IT!
- THE BETTER LOAFER
- PUTTING THE PLANET FIRST

**Product launch:**
- "NEW" ticker bar across top/bottom
- "INTRODUCING OUR SIGNATURE [PRODUCT]"

**Sale banners:**
- FLAT 50% OFF on [product line]
- USE CODE: [CODE]
- Red typography for studio shots, Red button for outdoor shots

### General Design Guidelines
1. Outdoor shots → **always paired with white or coal** (never color patches)
2. Text in free-flowing grids → flexible placement, but should **begin or end at margins**
3. Type height → **1x or 1.5x** height of a grid row
4. Single shoe closeup → can be used when product isn't focal point in hero image
5. Grids are **scalable** for flexibility in layouts & copy

## 13. CUSTOMER INSIGHTS

### Ratings
- **Judge.me (own site):** 4.7/5 (27,676 reviews)
- **MouthShut:** 2.2/5

### What People Love
- Ultra-lightweight, sock-free wearing
- Can wear 12+ hours without pain
- Value for money at current prices
- Minimal, versatile design
- Sustainability as a bonus

### Common Complaints
- **Sizing:** Runs small — common advice is to buy 1 size up
- **Some soles feel hard** — described as "hard plastic" on certain models
- **Customer service:** Slow, automated responses, poor human support
- **Returns:** Exchange-only policy, restrictive refund process, no refund on EMI
- **Initial discomfort:** Some footwear bites on first wear

### Key Insight for Creative
> Customers buy Neeman's primarily for **comfort + design + price**. Sustainability is a nice-to-have, not the purchase driver. Lead with comfort experience and style, layer sustainability as the "feel good" bonus.

## 15. QUICK REFERENCE — CREATIVE CHEAT SHEET

### When Writing Headlines
- Font: **Bebas Neue Pro Regular, ALL CAPS**
- Style: Conversational, curious, question-asking
- Lead with comfort experience or fashion appeal
- Layer sustainability as the feel-good factor
- Use spaced letterforms for impact: `A I R Y  C O M F Y  C O N S C I O U S`

### When Designing Ads
- **Studio shots** → brand color backgrounds + white/coal text patches
- **Outdoor shots** → ONLY white or coal backgrounds, no color patches
- **Product on feet** → 2 or 3 module grids
- **Model shots** → clothes match background color OR neutral black/white
- **Sale** → Red typography (studio) or red button (outdoor)
- **New launch** → "NEW" ticker bar + "INTRODUCING" format

### Brand Colors Quick Pick
- **Safe backgrounds:** Coal (#3A3A3A), Ecru (#F3F2EE), Wool (#EDD8C3)
- **E-commerce:** Wool or Pebble (#E9E1D6)
- **Accent (lifestyle feel):** Ocean, Forest, Sun
- **Sale/CTA:** Red

### Copy Formula
```
[Curiosity Hook / Question] + [Comfort/Fashion Benefit] + [Subtle Responsibility Layer]
```
Example: "Wouldn't it be great if doing good felt this good? Walk lighter. Look sharper. Change the norm."

### Voice Check
- [x] Does it sound like a conversation?
- [x] Would a curious explorer say this?
- [x] Is it confident but not aggressive?
- [x] Does it avoid cliches?
- [x] Does it paint a picture, not just describe?
- [x] Is it in US English?

## VIDEO GUIDELINES

### Logo in Video
- Start slate: Logo appears on blank screen, stays 1.5-2 seconds, fades out
- Options: Logo alone, or Logo → "CHANGE THE NORM" (1 sec) → fade
- Can appear on textured image background
- End slate: Blank → logo with tagline → website URL fade-in
- **Do NOT place logo in video body** — it already appears in social platform UI
- If absolutely necessary, place symbol/name in **top right corner**

### Logo Animation Styles
1. **Softness meets Comfort** — gentle fade-in (works with detailed symbol)
2. **Inspired by Travel** — letters animate as if moving on a path

### Frame Transitions
1. **Window to the World** — rounded square viewing window enlarges to full screen
2. **Bold/Edgy** — frame overlay transitions between scenes
3. **Mask shrink** — image masks into rounded square, reveals color behind
4. **Color swipe** — color patch enters from side, covers screen, exits other side

### Fonts in Video
- **Bebas Neue Pro Regular** — on colored backgrounds or minimal textured images only
- **Bebas Neue** (heavier) — can overlay on video, large font size, minimal text, can be used as outline
- **Roboto Regular** — animated ticker to encircle/frame videos (e.g., "NEW NEW NEW")
